ADA Compliance
Stay Compliant.
Avoid Fines.

ADA violations carry steep fines and can expose your business to lawsuits. We study the federal requirements and apply them carefully, by hand, every time.

  • Correctly-sized handicap stall widths (minimum 8 ft + 5 ft access aisle)
  • Proper ISA (International Symbol of Access) symbol placement
  • Van-accessible designations where required
  • Correct slope verification guidance and signage recommendations
  • Written documentation of completed ADA work available on request
